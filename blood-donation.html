Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a simple, safe, and rewarding process. Here are some reasons to donate:

  • One donation can save up to three lives.
  • Blood is needed every day for surgeries, emergencies, and chronic illnesses.
  • Donating blood is safe and takes less than an hour.
